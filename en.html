About Me

Born on March 29, 1983, in Odesa, Ukraine.

Professional Activity

Work Experience:

  • Discount Business Dept, Specialist for Corporate Clients (Pivdenny Bank, 2004).
  • Marketing Specialist, Marketing Policy Dept (Pivdenny Bank, 2005).
  • Head of Project Management Department (Pivdenny Bank, 2007).
  • CEO of Building Operations, "Marine Business Center" LLC (2008).
  • Chairman of the Board, "MBC" Group of Companies (since 2012).
  • Financial Advisor, "AXA Group" France (since 2012).
  • VIP Client Branch Manager (2013 – 2015).
  • Development Director, "Hillel IT School" (since 2025).
  • Founder, "Bracha Capitals" (since 2026).

Public & Local Government Activity:

  • Journalist for the "Economist" student newspaper, ONEU (2001).
  • Member of the Youth Council under the Odesa City Council (2003).
  • Head of the Odesa Regional Office, Ukrainian Association of Student Self-Government (2003).
  • Student Mayor of Odesa (2005-2006).
  • Chairman of the Board, "Hillel" Jewish Youth Center (since 2006).
  • Co-chairman of the Odesa Youth Council, Advisor to the Mayor (2008).
  • Member of the Odesa City Council (VI Convocation).
  • Chairman of the Standing Committee on Budget and Finance.
  • Founder of the "Week of Good Deeds" Charity Project (since 2010).
  • Deputy Mayor of Odesa (2016 – 2025).
  • Member of the Executive Committee, Odesa City Council (2016 – 2025).
  • Co-founder of the "Sportpark" in Starobazarnyi Square (2014).
  • Chairman of the Board, "City Basketball Federation" (since 2016).
  • Member of the Odesa District Council (I Convocation).
  • Chairman of the Supervisory Board, Innovation and Space Clusters Union of Ukraine.
  • Chairman of the Supervisory Board, UNION International Cultural Center.

Awards:

  • Honorary Award from the Mayor of Odesa "Gratitude", 2013.
  • National Recognition "Person of the Year - 2019" for "Odesa 5T" strategy.
  • Honorary Award "For Merits to the City", 2019.
