Pavlo Vugelman

Entrepreneur & Public Figure

Odesa, Ukraine

About Me

Born on March 29, 1983, in Odesa, Ukraine.

Education

  • Ukrainian Gymnasium № 2 with in-depth study of English (2000).
  • Bachelor of Economics, majoring in accounting and auditing (Odesa National University of Economics, 2004).
  • Bachelor of Law, Prosecutorial and Investigative Specialization (National University "Odesa Law Academy", 2004).
  • Master of Economics in Marketing (Odesa National University of Economics, 2005).
  • Specialist in jurisprudence, forensic specialization (National University "Odesa Law Academy", 2006).
  • Professional education in the international currency exchange market of Teletrade Consulting, 2003.
  • Graduate of the International Accounting Standards Program of the European Union and Ernst and Young Ukraine, 2004.
  • Diploma of Practical Management, Richard Ivey School of Business, The University of Western Ontario, 2005.
  • Graduate of the International Diplomatic Program for Young Leaders of the Ministry of International Relations, Israel, Jerusalem, 2008.
  • MBA, State University of Management, Institute of New Economics, M., 2009.
  • Alumnus of the “Emerging Leaders” program, Harvard Kennedy Business School, Harvard University, Cambridge, USA, 2017.
  • Master’s degree in Public Administration (National University "Odesa Law Academy", 2020).
  • Postgraduate student majoring in Management (Odesa Polytechnic State University, 2022).

Professional Activity

Work Experience:

  • Head of Project Management Department (Pivdenny Bank, 2004 – 2007).
  • CEO of Building Operations, Marine Business Center LLC (2008).
  • Chairman of the Board, MBC Group of Companies (since 2012).
  • Financial Advisor, AXA Group, France (since 2012).
  • VIP Client Branch Manager of the Bank (2013 – 2015).
  • Development Director, Hillel IT School (since 2025).
  • Founder, Bracha Capitals (since 2026).

Public & Local Government Activity:

  • Journalist for the Economist student newspaper, ONEU (2001).
  • Member of the Youth Council under the Odesa City Council (2003).
  • Head of the Odesa Regional Office, Ukrainian Association of Student Self-Government (2003).
  • Student Mayor of Odesa (2005–2006).
  • Chairman of the Board, Hillel Jewish Youth Center (since 2006).
  • Co-chairman of the Odesa Youth Council, Advisor to the Mayor (2008).
  • Member of the Odesa City Council (VI Convocation), Chairman of the Standing Committee on Budget and Finance.
  • Founder of the Week of Good Deeds Charity Project (since 2010).
  • Deputy Mayor of Odesa (2016 – 2025).
  • Member of the Executive Committee, Odesa City Council (2016 – 2025).
  • Co-founder of the Sportpark in Starobazarnyi Square (2014).
  • Chairman of the Board, City Basketball Federation (since 2016).
  • Member of the Odesa District Council (I Convocation).
  • Vice-President, Association of Innovation and Space Clusters.
  • Chairman of the Supervisory Board, UNION International Cultural Center.

Awards:

  • Honorary Award from the Mayor of Odesa, “Gratitude”, 2013.
  • People’s Recognition – Odessit of the Year (2019) for the creation of the Odesa 5T Investment Strategy.
  • Honorary Award from the Mayor of Odesa, “For Merits to the City”, 2019.
